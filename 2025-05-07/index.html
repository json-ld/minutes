Gregg Kellogg: That's great ✪

Gregg Kellogg is scribing.
Benjamin Young: I understand that CBOR-LD is not an LD format; it's just about compressing JSON-LD, you're supposed to round-trip through JSON-LD. ✪

Gregg Kellogg: Speaking on my own here, but we do call it CBOR-LD which makes it sound like an LD format ✪

Wesley Smith: I agree. It's likely an open question ✪
